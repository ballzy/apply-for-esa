4. Apply

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your partner's National Insurance number (if you have a partner)
  • details of any pensions, savings and income
  • details of any home insurance
  • details of any service charges you pay on your home
  • details of any doctors, nurses, counsellors or other people who help you
  • your bank or building society details
