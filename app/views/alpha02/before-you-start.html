Before you start

To apply for Employment and Support Allowance you must:

  • have a disability, health condition or illness
  • not be getting Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA), Universal Credit or Income Support
  • not be currently involved in industrial action (eg a strike, walk-out or lock-out)
  • not be working over 16 hours per week in either paid or voluntary work
