Before you start

This service is currently only available to people who have been invited to claim Employment and Support Allowance online.

If you have been invited enter your access code below.

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • your National Insurance number
  • the name, address and telephone number of your General Practitioner (GP)
  • your fit note if you have one (also known as a sick note or doctor’s note)

    A fit note describes how your condition affects your ability to work and is issued by a hospital doctor or GP.

  • hospital admission date if you’re currently in hospital
  • your employer’s address and telephone number
  • dates of employment
  • your last 2 payslips
  • form SSP1 if you have one

    Your employer should give you an SSP1 if you’re not entitled to Statutory Sick Pay (SSP), or when it’s coming to an end.

  • details of any income other than earnings or SSP
  • details of your money, savings or investments
  • bank, building society or post office account details

You’ll be asked to give consent for the Department of Work and Pensions to contact your GP (doctor). If you give consent you may withdraw it at any time.

Start now

The application will reset if you don’t do anything for more than 30 minutes - you’ll have to restart your application.
