Employment and Support Allowance: give details of your health conditions

This service is just a prototype for testing. No information is stored.

Use this service tell the Department for Work and Pensions how your health conditions affect you.

You can ask a friend, family member or support organisation to help you.

You can save your answers and return to them as often as you need in the next 28 days.

Before you start make sure you have details of:

  • medications you are taking
  • treatments or therapies you're getting or have planned
  • any healthcare professionals who know about your conditions
