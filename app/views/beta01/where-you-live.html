What is a 'service charge'?

A service charge is a payment made for the upkeep of the building your home is in.

You might be charged a service charge if you rent a property in a block of flats or in a housing development.

Sometimes the service charge is included in your rent. You can check your tenancy agreement if you’re not sure.
