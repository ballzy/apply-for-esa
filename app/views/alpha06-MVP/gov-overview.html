1. Overview

If you cannot work due to an illness or disability, Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) offers you:

  • money to help you while you’re unable to work
  • personalised support to help you prepare for work when you’re able to

If you are awarded Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) you’ll normally get a lower rate of ESA for the first 13 weeks of your claim.

This basic rate is up to:

  • £57.90 a week if you’re aged under 25
  • £73.10 a week if you’re aged 25 or over

You will be paid at these rates as long as you have up to date fit notes (used to be called sick notes) until after a decision is made on your ability to work.
