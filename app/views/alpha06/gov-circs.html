5. If your circumstances change

You must call if:

  • your personal details change
  • your medical condition or help you need changes
  • you or any partnerâ€™s work situation changes
  • the amount of money coming into your house changes
  • there are changes to who lives in your house
  • you are involved in a trade dispute, or cannot work because of a trade dispute
  • you go into or leave hospital
  • you go into prison or legal custody
  • you go abroad or are about to go abroad
  • your partner dies

If you do not tell us straight away then you could be paid more or less ESA than you should. You may have to pay back any money you are overpaid and pay a financial penalty or become involved in legal action.
