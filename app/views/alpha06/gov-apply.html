4. Apply

Before you apply make sure you have:

  • your National Insurance number
  • a fit note (also known as a sick note or doctor’s note)
  • your GP’s address and phone number
  • your home and mobile telephone numbers
  • your mortgage or landlord details
  • employer’s address and telephone number and dates of employment or last date worked
  • your bank, building society or post office account details
  • details of any money you are getting

Start now

You can’t register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.
