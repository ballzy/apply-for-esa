Send more information

Based on your answers, you must send your:

  • fit notes covering the weeks you're claiming for
  • medical reports
  • care or treatment plans
  • medical test results, including sight and hearing tests
  • current prescription list
  • Education, Health and Care plan (was ‘Statement of Special Needs')
  • recent bank statement

We cannot accept photocopies so send the original documents.

Write your National Insurance number on each page you send and post it before [date in four weeks].

If you do not send it before then, any ESA you are getting will be stopped and you might have to pay back what you’ve got so far.

Post to:

ESA
Mail Handling Site A
Wolverhampton
WV98 2AS

What happens next

You'll get a letter in the next 2 weeks. It will either:

  • tell you if you're eligible for ESA
  • ask for more information

You must report any change to your circumstances.

If you have any queries about your claim, phone 0345 850 3322. You’ll need your National Insurance number.
